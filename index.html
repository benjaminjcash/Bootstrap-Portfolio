About Me

I grew up in the small mountain town of Nederland, Colorado. After graduating high school in 2008, I attended CU Boulder and earned a Bachelor's degree in Mathematics. I am currently attending the DU coding bootcamp to start my career in development.
Traveling is my true love. My primary goal is to see as much of the planet as possible. I've spent many weeks backpacking the national parks that the western United States has to offer including Yosemite, Zion, and Glacier. Hopefully I'll be fortunate enough to see them all.
